Coaching is for general fitness and wellness and is **not medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment**. The Coach is not your physician. You should consult a qualified healthcare provider before starting any exercise or nutrition program, especially if you have a medical condition, are pregnant or nursing, take medication, or have any injury or symptom.

## 3. Health disclosure

You confirm that you have disclosed relevant health information (including allergies, injuries, medical conditions, and medications) truthfully, and that you will promptly tell your Coach if anything changes. ## 4. Assumption of risk

Physical exercise carries inherent risks, including injury. By continuing, you voluntarily accept those risks and agree that you participate at your own discretion and risk.

## 5. No guarantees

The Coach makes no guarantee of any specific outcome, weight change, or performance result. The plan is a starting point that your Coach will review and adjust over time.

## 6. Your data

Information you provide is used to build and adjust your plan and to communicate with you. It is handled per the platform's privacy policy.

## 7. Communication & scheduling

Coaching is delivered asynchronously and on the schedule described in your plan tier. Response times and included features vary by tier.

## 8. Acknowledgement

By typing your full name and continuing, you confirm that you have read and understood this agreement, that your health disclosures are accurate, and that you consent to begin coaching with **{{trainerName}}**.
